## Design Guidelines — LEIRE DENTAL Y CLÍNICA

### Brand feel
Clinical-modern, calm, trustworthy, and warm. Health-tech, not enterprise CRUD. Leire (the voice assistant) is the signature element — give her a distinct, friendly visual presence.

### Color
- Primary: a calm medical teal/cyan (e.g. #0FB8B0 / #0E9C95) — evokes dental/health, clean and modern.
- Accent: a soft warm coral or amber for highlights and "Leire is listening" states (e.g. #FF8A6B), used sparingly.
- Neutrals: near-white background (#F7FAFB), white surfaces, slate text (#1F2A37), muted gray secondary text (#64748B).
- Semantic: success green, warning amber, danger red — desaturated/soft, never harsh.

### Typography
- Sans-serif, clean and legible (system UI stack or Inter-like). Generous sizes for clinic use (often glanced at from a distance).
- Clear hierarchy: large bold section titles, medium-weight labels, comfortable body line-height.

### Layout & components
- Calendar-centric agenda as the home screen: day/week views, columns per professional, color-coded appointment blocks.
- Persistent **Leire voice widget**: a floating mic button / orb. States: idle, listening (pulsing coral ring + waveform), thinking, speaking. Show live transcription text near the orb.
- Rounded corners (8–16px), soft shadows, airy spacing. Cards for appointments and patient summaries.
- Role-aware navigation sidebar; hide/disable items the role can't access.

### Elevation & motion
- Subtle elevation on cards and the voice orb. Gentle, organic animation for the listening state (breathing pulse), never jittery.
- Smooth transitions for opening appointment detail panels.

### Accessibility & language
- All UI text in Spanish (Spain). High contrast for text, large touch targets (clinic gloves / quick glances).
- Voice and visual feedback always paired — when Leire speaks, also show the text.
