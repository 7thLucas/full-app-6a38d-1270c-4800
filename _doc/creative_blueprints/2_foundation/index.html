Foundation Validated

Manual scheduling, patient data entry, and clinical notes disrupt workflow and demand constant context-switching away from patient care.

The Problem

Healthcare professionals lose productivity to administrative overhead. Dentists and aesthetic specialists spend up to 40% of their day on scheduling, patient records, and note-taking—tasks that pull focus from direct patient care and increase clinical errors.

The Solution

Sistema Leire replaces keyboard-bound administration with a voice-first assistant. Say "Leire" and the system transcribes the request, acts, and responds in natural Spanish — instantly, hands-free. A single integrated platform covers agenda, patient records, billing, inventory, and reporting, deployable local, on a LAN, or over the internet with mobile app access.

Target Audience

Dentists, aesthetic specialists, clinic directors, multi-location networks, and healthcare administrators seeking hands-free, integrated clinical management.

Personal Motivation

Reclaim clinical focus by moving routine administration into the background. Leire is the invisible colleague that keeps operations running smoothly so your team can concentrate on patient care.
